Frequently Asked Questions

Is DRAZZE's activity legal? +

Yes. Drazze Limited has an iGaming license from the Anjouan (Union of Comoros) regulator. You can verify this information yourself in the official register.

What is a 'deflationary model' for a token? +

Every 6 months, mining decreases by 1%. Part of the profit goes to buying back and burning tokens, creating scarcity and increasing their value.

What protects the project from dumps and manipulation? +

DRZ economics is built on limited emission, deflationary model, reduced mining, buyback and burning, and real token usage. This reduces speculative pressure and shifts focus to long-term value.

Why was iGaming chosen? +

It is one of the most profitable and stable industries in the world with constant growth in turnover.

What are revenue pools? +

A mechanism for distributing part of iGaming profits among participants.
